    About

    • The KL University in Vijayawada boasts a vibrant Dramatics Club, serving as a creative haven for students passionate about the performing arts. This dynamic club within the academic landscape fosters talent, offering a platform for aspiring actors, directors, and theater enthusiasts. Through engaging workshops, auditions, and full-scale productions, the KL University Dramatics Club encourages artistic expression and teamwork. It plays a pivotal role in enhancing students' holistic development, nurturing not only acting skills but also instilling a sense of camaraderie. In Vijayawada, the Dramatics Club stands as a testament to the university's commitment to fostering creativity and a love for the stage.

    Events

    Mono Action

    • Embark on a solo journey of expression at Surabhi Fest's Mono Action Competition, where the stage transforms into a canvas for individual storytelling. This riveting event celebrates the art of monologues, inviting participants to captivate the audience with their solo performances. From heartfelt narratives to intense character portrayals, Mono Action is a showcase of raw talent and emotional resonance. Each participant becomes a storyteller, weaving a compelling narrative that leaves a lasting impact. Surabhi Fest's Mono Action Competition is a testament to the power of a single performer, creating a space where individual stories take center stage, leaving the audience moved and inspired.

    SKIT

    • Embrace the limelight at Surabhi Fest's Skit Competition, where creativity knows no bounds. This riveting event invites teams to weave stories, evoke laughter, and ignite emotions through compelling performances. As the stage transforms into a canvas for theatrical expression, participants showcase their acting prowess, scriptwriting skills, and impeccable timing. Surabhi Fest's Skit Competition is a celebration of diverse narratives, from thought-provoking dramas to sidesplitting comedies. Join this exhilarating platform where talent takes center stage, fostering camaraderie among participants and leaving the audience spellbound. It's more than a competition; it's an opportunity to bring stories to life and leave an indelible mark on Surabhi Fest.

    Mimicry

    • Surabhi Fest beckons you to the Mimicry Competition, a riotous rendezvous of vocal virtuosos. Participants, armed with uncanny impersonation skills, breathe life into an array of characters, celebrities, and iconic voices. This uproarious event transcends imitation, embracing the art of mimicry as a form of entertainment. From spot-on accents to comedic caricatures, the stage becomes a playground for vocal dexterity. The Mimicry Competition at Surabhi Fest is a symphony of laughter and admiration, where participants showcase their extraordinary ability to mirror and embody diverse personalities. Join in the fun, and let the echoes of mimicry reverberate through the festival halls.

    Dialogue Dhamaka

    • Dive into the linguistic extravaganza at Surabhi Fest's Dialogue Dhamaka Competition, a thrilling showcase of verbal prowess. This unique event invites participants to unleash the power of words, engaging in dynamic conversations that captivate and resonate. From witty banter to impassioned debates, competitors navigate linguistic landscapes with flair, wit, and charm. Dialogue Dhamaka is more than a competition; it's a celebration of the art of conversation, where eloquence meets entertainment. Participants spar verbally, leaving audiences enthralled and sparking lively discussions. Join this linguistic battleground at Surabhi Fest, where every dialogue is a detonation of linguistic brilliance.
